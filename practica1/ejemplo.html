Pator Aleman

The German Shepherd, also known in Britain as an Alsatian, is a German breed of working dog of medium to large size. The breed was developed by Max von Stephanitz using various traditional German herding dogs from 1899.

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed of retriever dog of medium size. It is characterised by a gentle and affectionate nature and a striking golden coat. It is a working dog, and registration is subject to successful completion of a working trial.

Chihuahua



The Chihuahua is a Mexican breed of toy dog. It is named for the Mexican state of Chihuahua and is the smallest dog breed in the world. It is usually kept as a companion animal.

Beagle



The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting rabbit or hare, known as beagling.

Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is a breed of retriever gun dog. It was originally bred by the fishermen of Newfoundland to help retrieve fishing nets and catch fish that escaped from fishing lines.

Bulldog


The Bulldog is a medium-sized breed of dog, known for its loose skin, stocky body, and distinctive pushed-in face. Bulldogs are friendly, calm, and good with families.

Dachshund

The Dachshund is a breed of dog known for its long body and short legs. Originally bred to hunt small burrowing animals, dachshunds are popular companion dogs due to their quirky personalities.

Husky



The Siberian Husky is a breed of medium-sized working sled dog. The breed belongs to the Spitz genetic family.
